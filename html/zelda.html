About

Link, a commoner, is suddenly thrust into a mysterious, magical quest in the kingdom of Hyrule to save the princess, Zelda, from one evil or another.

Characters

  • Link - Link is a fictional character and the main protagonist of Nintendo's video game series The Legend of Zelda, He is one of Nintendo's main icons.

  • Gannon - Ganon is a fictional character and the main antagonist of Nintendo's The Legend of Zelda video game series.

  • Princess Zelda - Princess Zelda is the titular character in Nintendo's The Legend of Zelda video game series.

  • Impa - Impa is a Sheikah in the The Legend of Zelda series.

  • Tingle - Tingle is the video game character of the eponymous Tingle series. He was originally part of The Legend of Zelda series

  • Midna - Midna is a fictional character introduced as one of the main protagonists in The Legend of Zelda

  • Epona - Epona is a fictional horse in The Legend of Zelda series of video games that debuted in The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
