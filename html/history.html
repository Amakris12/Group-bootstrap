The Beginning

The wii was released in November 19th 2006 and it became one of the most popular gaming systems that has ever lived. The first game "Wii Sports" that came out is still an extremly popular game today. Its original release price was $249.99.

As the wii came out popularity came along with it, in the first few years almost everyone that could afford it, had the Wii console. They head multiple gaming hits with Mario Kart, Legend of Zelda, Mario Party, and more. At this time the Wiis popularity was at its highest but still growing.

From January 2007 to may 2010 was the peak of the Wii and it had many great games during the console. It may have been the only current gen console at the time but it was still an amazing system. It had many great hits for games and is still popular till this very day

The fall of the wii happened as the Ps3 and the Xbox 360 came out since they were more technologicly advance than the Wii and could play a different set of games. The wii was still highly popular than this time though

There technically has not been an end to the Wii gaming system because they are still played on and you can still purchase them from big name story brands. After the Wii, nintendo came out with the Wii U which is just a more advanced version of the Wii with better online capabilities. The wii is still around today and doest look to be going anywhere soon.
